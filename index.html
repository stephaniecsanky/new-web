About

I am a Canadian Graphic Designer based in Ottawa, Ontario. A graduate of Algonquin College's Advanced Diploma program in Graphic Design along with a completed Certificate in Design Studies, I have experience in web design, graphic design and print. Furthermore, I am proficient with Microsoft Office programs as well as Google Drive, which I use on a daily basis. In my spare time I enjoy travelling, video games, reading and spending time with family.

Since childhood, I've had a love for the arts which started off with childhood arts & crafts, painting, drawing and making my own newsletters. As I grew older I matured more into design software and chose to follow my passion through to college. I'm currently seeking long-term employment with a design agency or company that could utilize my extensive web & graphic design skills.

Skills

With an extensive amount of my lifetime spent on computers and also studying graphic design, I have come to be proficient in a variety of programs along with establishing a handful of skills. Programs I am proficient in are: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Dreamweaver, After Effects, Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel and Powerpoint.
